In this Sci-fi comedy adventure, director Robert Zemeckis invites you on the journey of a lifetime. High school student Marty McFly is an average 1985 highschool student, with dreams of becoming a rockstar. That is until he is acidentally sent 30 years into the past by a time travelling Delorean built by mad scientist Dr. Emmett Brown. Now with only one chance of returning to his own time and his very existance in danger, will Marty find his way Back to the Future?
